1. Python : The famous programming language of the world

The First Inventor of Python

Python is one of the most popular programming languages in the world. It was first released in 1991 by Guido van Rossum.

1. Guido van Rossum is the creator of Python.
2. Born on 31 January 1956 in the Netherlands.
3. Started Python in December 1989 at CWI.
4. Wanted a language that was simple, readable, and powerful.
5. Named it after Monty Python’s Flying Circus.
6. First version released in 1991.
7. Worked at Google, Dropbox, etc.
8. Known as Python’s Benevolent Dictator For Life (BDFL).
9. Stepped down in 2018.
10. His creation changed programming forever.
